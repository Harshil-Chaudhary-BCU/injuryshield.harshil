- Take a look at different powerlifting exercises

Squat

The squat is a compound movement targeting the muscles of the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. It involves bending the knees and hips to lower the body down and then standing back up while supporting a weighted barbell across the shoulders.

Dead Lift

The deadlift is a full-body exercise focusing on the muscles of the back, hips, and legs. It requires lifting a weighted barbell from the ground to a standing position while maintaining a straight back and braced core.

Bench Press

The bench press primarily targets the muscles of the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It involves lying on a flat bench and pushing a weighted barbell upwards from the chest to full arm extension.
